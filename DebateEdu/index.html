The Captsone Journey

Our Progressive learning system adapts to each debater's growth, with clear advancement pathways based on demonstrated skill masterty.

Introductory Level

Your Debate journey begins here. After an initial assessment interview, students develop fundamental skills including:

  • Core logical reasoning and argument construction.
  • Essential public speaking and stage presence.
  • Introduction to World Schools formats.

Intermediate Level

As debaters progress, they dive deeper into competitive debate:

  • First tournament experiences at local competitions.
  • Advanced argument analysis and strategic thinking.
  • Research methodology and case building skills.
  • Introduction to British Parliamentary format.

Advanced Level

Experienced debaters tackle complex challenges:

  • Regional and international tournament opportunities.
  • Advanced topics in Philosophy, Geopolitics and Economics.
  • Sophisticated argument framing and delivery.
  • Mastery of speaker roles and positions.

Varsity Team

Our elite program represents Capstone's highest level of training. Many team members have progressed to represent Hong Kong, and other territories, nationally. Selection is based on:

  • Performance in our internal ranking system
  • Sucecess in internal and external competitions
  • Dedicated team trials

With hundreds of accomplished debaters across our centers, our Varsity Team stand among world's strongest youth debate programs.
